About Me

I am a Software Engineer at BJIT Limited and a research intern at Carnegie Mellon University. Prior to this, I received my B.Sc.Engg. in Computer Science and Engineering from East West University, Bangladesh. During my undergrad, I worked with Mozammel H A Khan on algorithm development.


Currently, I am using Computer Vision methods to in search of novel macromolecules from 3D cryo-electron tomography (cryo-ET) images of Sars-Cov-2 virus under the supervision of Dr. Min Xu and Mostofa Rafid Uddin. My primary research interests are,
  1. Computer Vision
  2. Computational Biology
